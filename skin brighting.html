Lemon juice,Honey & Milk

  • Mix together one tablespoon milk,one teaspoon honey and one tablespoon lemon juice
  • Apply the mixture on your cleansed face
  • Let the mixture sit on your face for about 20 minutes before rinsing with warm water

    • Potato juice

  • Peel one potato and cut it into very small pieces.
  • Rub the potato pieces on the areas that your want to lighten so that the juice spreads onto the skin.
  • Leave it on for about 20 minutes before rinsing with likewarm water.
  • An alternative to the above procedure is that you can grate a potato,squeeze out the juice and apply it on the affected area with the help of a cotton pad.

    • Papaya & honey

  • Take half cup fresh papaya pieces and mash until you get a smooth paste.
  • Add one teaspoon honey to the paste and mix well.
  • Apply the pack on your face and leave it on for about 20 minutes.
  • Wsh your face with warm water and follow it up with a cold-water rinse.
  • Pat your skin dry.

    • Yoghurt

  • Mix two teaspoons plain yoghurt with one teaspoon honey.
  • Mix the ingredients well to form a smooth paste.
  • Apply the mixture on your face and let it sit for 15 minutes.
  • Rinse your face with cool water.

    • Rice flour & Milk

  • Grind half cup raw rice and make sure you get a fine powder.
  • Add 3-4 tablespoons milk to the powder to form a paste.
  • Apply the paste all over your face and leave it on your atleast half an hour.
  • Wash your face with warm water.

    • Cumin(jeera) seeds

  • Boil one teaspoon cumin seeds in two cups of water.
  • Wash your face with this concoction.

    • Sandalwood(chandan) powder

  • Mix one tablespoon sandalwood powder and almond powder each.
  • Add some milk to form a smooth paste.
  • Apply the mixture on clean skin and leave it on for about 20 minutes.
  • Rinse with lukewarm water.if any area of your skin feels dry after washing,then moisturise well.

    • Crrot & Avocado

  • Boil one medium-sizrd carrot and once it is cooled,mash it along with the pulp of one avocado
  • Add one tablespoon heavy cream,one egg and one tablespoon honey to the mixture.
  • Mix the ingredients well to form a thick,smooth paste.
  • Apply the pack on your face and neck evenly.
  • Let the mixture sit on your face for 15 minutes before rinsing off with cool water.
