Garlic paste

  • Crush the cloves of garlic using one mortar and pestle.
  • Then, add this garlic paste with honey and olive oil.
  • After that, apply this paste on the ringworm affected skin.
  • Allow for about one hour.
  • Rinse off with warm water.
  • You should repeat 2-3 times per day for at least two weeks.

    • Garlic

  • Take a few cloves of garlic and next peel it off
  • Cut this garlic into many thin slices and then place it in your ringworm.
  • Cover this place with band-aid or gauze and leave it for an entire night.
  • In the next morning, remove this and continue this process regularly to remove the ringworm.

    • Apple Cider Vinegar

    Ingredients: Hand towel or cotton ball Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Firstly, clean your affected skin area and then pat dry.
  • Before using apple cider vinegar on the rashes, you should do one patch test to see if the skin is sensitive to this vinegar.
  • After that, apply some vinegar with a cotton ball on the ringworm rashes. You should repeat 3-4 times a day.

    • Raw Papaya

  • Take one fresh raw papaya and next separate the seeds from the papaya.
  • Put these seeds under the sunlight for around three to four hours to get dry
  • Then, crush these dried seeds and then add water to create it like one paste.
  • Now, apply this paste on your infected area
  • Allow it on for around 15 – 20 minutes and rinse it off
  • You had better do the process for about 2 – 3 times per day to remove the ringworm.

    • Raw papaya Method-2

  • First of all, take one raw papaya and next to cut it into many small slices
  • After that, take one of the slices and rub it on your ringworm patches
  • If required use another slice simultaneously to rub it on your ringworm infected area for around 10 – 15 minutes
  • It is suggested to do this process three times per day regularly to prevent the ringworm as well as its infection completely.

    • Salt

    Method-1

  • Add salt (¼ to ½ cup) to one basin filled with warm water. Then soak your ringworm affected area in the salty warm water for about half an hour.

    Method-2

  • you can take little sea salt and then use a little water, and moisten it a little. Now use the salt scrub to rub into the ringworm using a washcloth. You should do this around 15-20 minutes every day.

    • Turmeric

    Method-1

  • Grind or crush the turmeric root to create its paste. You can use some water if needed.
  • Then, apply this turmeric paste on the ringworm.
  • Keep for about 15-20 minutes.
  • Rinse off with warm water and mild soap because turmeric tends to stain.
  • Another option is to apply a few drops of turmeric oil on your infection.
  • After that, take the mixture orally whenever applying this turmeric juice on your ringworm affected area to prevent the problem quickly and easily.

    • Turmeric Juice

  • Allow it like that to remove the ringworm effectively.
  • In the meanwhile, take one teaspoon of both raw honey and turmeric juice.
  • Stir it well as both must be properly mixed
  • A regular repeat of the process helps you a lot to remove the ringworm.

    • Aloevera

    • Camphor(Karpura)

    Method 1

  • Take camphor powder (a pinch) and apply it every day to your ringworm.

    • Method 2

  • You can mix it with some sesame oil and then rub your rashes with the oil. You can repeat two times a day.

    • Method 3

  • You can dilute camphor oil (2-3 drops), 1-2 tablespoons of some carrier oil such as olive oil or sweet almond and apply to the ringworm 2-3 times per day.

    • Neem

    Method 1

    • Method 2

    • Method 3

  • You can dilute neem oil (1 teaspoon) with aloe vera gel (½ a cup) and then apply this to the ringworm 2-3 times per day.

    • Mustard Seeds

  • Take few seeds of mustard and then crush them to get powder form
  • After that, add water (few drops) and create it like one fine paste.
  • Now, apply this paste on your ringworm affected area.
  • Leave it for around 45 minutes to one hour.
  • Wash it off with lukewarm water.

    • Mustard Seeds Paste

  • You can soak a few seeds of mustard in water for around half an hour.
  • Grind it to create a thick paste and then apply this on your infected area to get relief from the irritation and itching as well.
