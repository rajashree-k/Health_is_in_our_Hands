Symptoms that may occur along with weight gain

  • Abnormal menstrual cycle
  • Constipation
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Hair loss or abnormal hair development
  • Malaise or lethargy
  • Shortness of breath
  • Swelling in the face, abdomen or extremities
  • Temperature sensitivity (especially to cold)
  • Vision changes

Serious symptoms that may occur along with weight gain

  • Absent or diminished pulses
  • Rapid heart rate
  • Rapid swelling (edema) in the face, arms, legs, feet, ankles or abdomen
  • Rapid weight gain
  • Seizure
  • Severe headache
  • Shortness of breath
  • Liver failure
  • Heart failure
  • Growth hormone deficiency

    Digestive causes of weight gain

  • Liver disease or failure
  • Pancreatic islet cell tumor
