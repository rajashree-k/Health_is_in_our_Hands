    • Cumin seed/Jeerige Kashayam

  • Heat a pan and add in 2 tbsp cumin seeds or jeerige.
  • Roast the cumin seeds until they start crackling nicely under high flame.
  • Immediately reduce the flame and add 2 cups water. You should be quick and make sure that the cumin seeds are not burnt.
  • Bring it to boil and continue boiling for 5 minutes under low flame.
  • Wait for few minutes (the cumin will settle down at the bottom). You need to drink this hot. So pour the kashaya into the cups. You can either strain it or just pour it slowly. We will be discarding the cumin.
