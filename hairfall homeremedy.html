Olive Oil

  • First, mix olive oil and coconut oil well in the bowl.
  • Next, apply a small amount of the mixture to your scalp and hair
  • Then, gently massage your hair in 15 minutes. Be careful that you should not pull the hair while massaging because it may increase the amount of hair fall.
  • After that, cover your head with the shower cap. Let the mixture stay on your hair overnight or at least 4 hours.
  • In the next morning, rinse your hair off with shampoo and water.
