    • Tulsi face pack for skin brightening

  • Take around 10 tulsi leaves and half a teaspoonful of milk powder.
  • Grind the tulsi leaves with some water and add the milk powder to form a paste of medium consistency.
  • Apply this paste on the face and let it dry for 20 minutes.
  • Rinse the face with plain water.
  • This tulsi face pack is excellent for brightening the skin.
